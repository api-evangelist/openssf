<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
	<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In this inaugural episode of </span><b>Big Thoughts, Open Sources</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">, host CRob sits down with Brian Fox, Co-founder and CTO of Sonatype, to discuss the friction between rapid AI adoption and foundational software security. Brian shares insights from the 11th annual State of the Software Supply Chain Report, revealing the emergence of &#8220;slop squatting&#8221; and the high frequency of AI models recommending non-existent or vulnerable dependencies. The conversation explores how the Model Context Protocol (MCP) could revolutionize developer compliance and why the industry must fund the critical infrastructure supporting our trillion-dollar open source ecosystem.</span></p>
