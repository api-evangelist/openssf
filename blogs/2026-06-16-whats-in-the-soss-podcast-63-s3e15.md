A discussion featuring Jamie Thomas from IBM on how enterprises can shift from accidentally consuming open source to intentionally stewarding it. The episode covers IBM's historical investment in Linux and Java, supply chain security lessons from incidents like SolarWinds and Log4j, and practical strategies for organizational give-back approaches to open source participation.
