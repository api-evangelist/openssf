June highlighted the high stakes for open source security. The European Open Source Security Forum focused on turning CRA commitments into action, while the Mini Shai-Hulud and Miasma threats underscored the need for strong provenance. Despite these challenges, the community progressed with new machine-readable guidance, a SLSA supply chain post-mortem, and a critical CRA Awareness report.
